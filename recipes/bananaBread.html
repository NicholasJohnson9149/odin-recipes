Banana Bread Recipe

Preheat Oven to 350F and use a 6x9in bread pan with butter to greese it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or 8tbs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs well beaten
  • 1 tsp of vinella
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup nuts or chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Butter pan
  2. Mix sugar, butter, eggs, and vinella in large bowl
  3. Add baking soda, and bananas
  4. Mix flour in slowly
  5. Add nuts or chocolate chips or both
  6. Add mix to pan and place in oven

Back for 45 to 60 minutes or until a knife pull out clean, then remove from pan onto a rack to cool for 5 miuntes then serve.
